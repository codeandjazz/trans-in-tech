About us

Who are we?

We are musicians, developers, drag artists, ravers, night owls, morning people and overall awesome human beings. What we all have in common is that we are trans and we do something tech-related professionally.

Languages we know:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Python
  • JSX

What is our mission?

We want to inspire each other to think outside the box and learn new things. We want to uplift each other in times of need. And we want to collaborate with each other, as well as with our allies.

Things we believe in:

  • Knowledge sharing
  • Learning by doing
  • Taking breaks

What are we currently doing?

When we are not busy resting or have other more important to-do's, we hold workshops for each other every week on Tuesdays.

Things we have learned about so far:

  • TouchDesigner

Would you like to join?

We welcome any new members, whether newbies or seasoned nerds, to join TIT. The only criteria being that you identify as trans in some kind of way, and that you are doing something tech-related.

Things we would love to learn more about:

  • Data visualization
  • Freelancing
  • SVG
