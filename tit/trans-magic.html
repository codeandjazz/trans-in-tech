Trans Magic

What was the process like?

With the feel of the venue in mind, my process was pretty intuitive, using the audiovisual production software TouchDesigner. I started out with making some generative textures, by feeding a circle with noise signals and processing it with different filters. Then I applied a video of a waterfall I recorded a few years ago, also processed in various filters.

What was the hardest part?

The hardest part was to make the visual composition fit with the room. I had to rearrange the composition a couple of times before it fit perfectly with both the position of the projector and the surfaces it was projecting onto. What looked fine on my computer screen looked awful in the room, and vice versa.

What was the result?

At the beginning the visuals were pretty dominated by blackness and edges with a high focus on detail, but since this didn’t work out very well in the projector format I had to put quite some time into reworking the textures, using different layers and rearranging the color schemes. The end result turned out to have a way more underwater feel than I intended to, but I like it.
